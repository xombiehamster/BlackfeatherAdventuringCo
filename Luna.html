Luna

Luna

True Neutral

    It is theorized that Luna, at one time, may have been a Major Deity, but is not considered such at the time. For many generations, the Lycanthropes have worshiped Luna. Up until recently, most thought that Lycanthropes literally worshipped the moon itself. While this is not completely inaccurate, it is more accurate to say that they worship Luna, and Luna happens to be the moon, among many things.

    Luna is traditionally depicted as a wispy feminine humanoid figure of mysterious features. She is the moon, and thus controls the night sky, as well as her chosen children the Lycanthropes. While most consider Luna a motherly figure, she is also a force of nature and capable of all the viciousness and callousness that this entails.

    Unfortunately for the Lycans, no one has managed to make contact with Luna in  many generations. She has not made a personal appearance in the world in over a thousand years. This doesn't seem to bother them too much however, as one put it: "Of course I can see her, she's right up there!"


    While it would seem that Luna is a fairly benign deity, this is not entirely true. She represents the many-formed nature of her flock. She has three forms, that of a humanoid female, a pale-luminous female wolf, and as the moon itself. As a humanoid, she is kind, gentle, soothing, and peaceful. As the moon, she is impassive, distant, and powerful. As a wolf, she is the Huntress. Lycanthropes have always hunted on a full moon, and it is a time of power, worship, and violence throughout the history of Lycanthropes.


    The ALT has toned down the violence aspect, but large organized hunts are still common on full moons. The Winter Solstice, in particular, is viewed as a holy day. A Full Moon on a winter solstice (Which only happens about once every twenty years) is a major event, and usually the center of a nationwide religious celebration. Several intriguing and beneficial events have happened in the ALT on the Winter Solstice (including finding one of the world's largest veins of Mithril,) leading some to theorize that the location of their new home was blessed by Luna.


    Worship of Luna tends to be decentralized. Though several temples exist in ALT territory, most worship happens in forest clearings or druid groves, usually led by druids or oracles. These are less organized religious institutions as they are community centers, and the religious leaders are guides, therapists, and consultants for their flock. Groves to Luna frequently take the form of simple observatories, dedicated to watching and charting the movements of the stars and moon. Many Lycan scholars suggest that their people were the first to truly chart the movements of the heavens. Given that some observers have stone calendars that are thousands of years old and still accurate, some modern scholars are reluctantly inclined to agree with them.


    In an important note, Silver is considered an unholy metal, and Mithril is considered a holy metal to Lycanthropes. Mithril, due to its pale color, is seen as condensed moonlight, Luna's grace brought down from the heavens. Silver is seen as a corrupted form of Mithril, twisted and turned against Luna's children (much like the Affliction itself.) It was only recently that it was even legal to bring silver into the ALT, and even now most Lycan establishments won't take silver. Buying or selling silver weapons is illegal. Possessing silver weapons is technically legal, though those who do are viewed with extreme distrust.


Preferred Weapon: Natural Attacks

Domains

Animal, Darkness, Plant, Dream

    Luna is considered the Goddess of the Night Skies, as well as many natural creatures (Especially Lycanthropes, and the types of creatures related to them) and plants. Sleep and dreams also fall under her purview. Theoretically, at least.

Subdomains

Night, Moon, Stars

    The Moon in particular is held as a holy symbol for Luna, and anything related to the night sky is seen as an expression of her.
