Lowbloods

   The primary Serpente that most people see in the overworld are lowbloods, those that have two legs and a tail. The vast majority of merchants and adventurers come from this more socially flexible class.

    Serpente lowbloods are slightly shorter than humans, and tend to have very thin builds. They are entirely covered in scales. Serpente scales tend to be vibrantly colored, but earth tones are also common. Scale patterns are extremely diverse. Serpente faces have something of a mix of snakelike features and a touch of draconic- a sign of their heritage. Since Serpente don't share many of the secondary sex characteristics of other races (such as breasts or beards) it can be difficult for outsiders to tell the difference between genders. Serpente have considerably more varied muscle groups, considerably more cartilage in their bones, and considerable more range of motion in their joints than other species. Lifespan for the average Serpente lowblood is only slightly more than a human. They are not cold-blooded, but still generally dislike cold environments.



Size: Medium

Abilities: Str-2, Dex +2, Cha +2- Serpente are agile and cosmopolitan, but they lack the brute force of other races.

Movement: Speed 30, Climb 20, Swim 20

Senses: Lowlight vision

Natural armor and weapons: +1 Natural armor, 1d4 bite


Special:
Keen Senses: +2 perception


Serpent Bodied: +2 stealth, +4 acrobatics - Serpente have extremely agile bodies. They can use dexterity to modify their climb and swim checks- they can use their relatively light weight and extreme agility to great advantage.


Snake Sense: Serpente have the ability to sense temperature differences and taste pheromones. They receive a +2 bonus to perception checks to detect living creatures within 30 feet. They can take Keen Scent as a feat if they have wisdom 13 or better.


Alternate Racial traits-

Silverscale Trader- This serpente lowblood has been trained by the silverscale trading company. They are required to know how to defend themselves, as well as how to swindle and dazzle with fast talk. They gain proficiency with a single firearm- either pistol, coat pistol, or musket. They gain a +1 on Bluff and Diplomacy, gain an extra language when they put a rank in linguistics, and if they succeed on a diplomacy check to influence attitude, their target takes a -2 to attempts to resist all charisma-based skills from that serpente for 24 hours. This trait replaces serpent bodied.
