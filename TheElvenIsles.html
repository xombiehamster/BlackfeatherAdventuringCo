The Elven Isles

    The traditional birthplace (although not necessarily the historically accurate one) of Elvenkind, the Elven Isles is a large range of Volcanic Isles ranging in size from small continent to atoll, all densely clustered together in an archipelago off the west coast of the Mainland Continent.  A curious feature of these isles is a great diversity in ecosystems, terrain features, flora, and fauna, often times featuring climates that seem impossible to sustain given their location.  While powerful magical alterations do account for some of these, the vast majority seem to be the result of geological shapes, rain shadows, and wind breaks that are likely too complex to have arisen naturally; the origin of these unusual formations is hotly debated.  Nonetheless, the result of this wide variety of ecosystems has had on elven physiology is plain to see for anyone with a cursory knowledge of the various elven subtypes.

    Elves from the Isles have long been held as the inventors of the greatest arcane achievements in history.  This is not without good reason.  As the Isles have extremely limited natural resources, specifically in the area of usable metals, they instead have historically focused their inventiveness on the use of magic.

    Cities in the Isles tend to be dense, relatively small of footprint compared to cities of similar population levels on the mainland.  They also tend to be built largely of wood and stone, with enormous wooden structures only made possible by magical alterations to the trees used in construction.  Many of these buildings, including some of the largest among them, are actually shaped from living trees.
