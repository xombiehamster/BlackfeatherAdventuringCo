Emperyan

EMPERYAN

True Neutral
Portfolio: Magic, Elemental forces, The Astral, Space, Mysteries, Emotionlessness
Domains: Air,Earth, Fire, Water, Magic, Void
Subdomains: Arcane, Divine, Dark Tapestry, Stars\

Preferred Weapon:

Emperyan, god of magic, is the aloof and cold overseer of all things Thaumaturgic. Emperyan has little love for those that worship it; it cares not for mortal concerns. Unlike the other gods, Emperyan lays claim to no Chosen; Sustained by the Vast Native Arcane power of The World, it has no need for worshippers. Rather, mortals of great ambition, intelligence, or mental fortitude will seek Emperyan, for there is great power to be found in his worship. Those who would court Emperyan for power are best advised to think of the god less as a patron to be appeased, and more as a force to be contended with: great power flows unceasingly from the god of Magic, available only to those who have the Will or Knowhow to control it. More than one of his petitioners has found themselves in over their heads: much of the fallout from the ancient Wizard War remains as a testament to the danger of this path. Still, mortals are nothing if not foolhardy in the quest for ultimate power, and every year thousands more will seek the attention of Emperyan.

For all their constant pleas and platitudes, Emperyan is not irked or annoyed by its mortal supplicants. Indeed, the great god feels nothing toward them. Its is a vast and unfathomable intelligence, a font of unbelievable power, a facet of existence; to stoop so low as to experience emotion is anathema to it. Instead, Emperyan grants power to those it calculates as worthy; its policy for worthiness being as inscrutable as the rest of the god. Just as it claims no Chosen, Emperyan offers no Gift: Yet, to many, the Gift of Emperyan is as clear as can be.
