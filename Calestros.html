Calestros

CALESTROS

Chaotic Neutral

Portfolio: Elves, Art, Chaos, Health, Seaships, Secrets, Insanity, Pride
Domains: Chaos, Charm, Healing, Madness, Trickery, Water
Sub-Domains: Restoration, Resurrection, Insanity, Deception, Oceans

Preferred Weapon: Longbow

Calestros, the Patron of Elvenkind, has always had a grand plan for her chosen. Seeing that the Elves were unlike other races, possessed of a dignity and capacity for Beauty that far outstripped their peers, Calestros fawned over her chosen, giving them everything they desired. The Archipelago, from which the Elves originated, is a place of great beauty and serenity: Under the guidance of Calestros, they flourished, spreading far and wide to the nearby isles. In those times, however, the Elves did not have their vaunted lifespans: Like Dwarves, the passing of a Century or two was all their frail bodies could handle. Calestros was displeased with this, and hated to see her followers suffer the agony of death so quickly. Out of Love for her chosen, Calestros searched the untamed knowledges of the Greater Unknown for the secret to mortal immortality, a truth long forbidden for even gods to know. Although she ultimately did find this Secret Lore, the revelation of it drove Calestros mad, shattering her psyche. As she spread Longevity to the Elves, so too did she spread her madness: Once a unified people, the Elves turned on themselves, fractioning and dividing. From one race came the Wood Elves, the Gray Elves, the Drow, the High Elves, and many more, each of these people a reflection upon the broken mirror of Calestros’ mind. The Elves stood in fear of their insane goddess: the loving guidance of the Calestros they had once known was no more. However, in spite of her Madness, Calestros had one final gift to give to her chosen, for in spite of her insanity, she still felt love for them. Pride, the Gift of Calestros, assuaged the anguish of the Elves, for even though their society crumbled around them, even though their protector had succumed to madness, they knew in their hearts that Calestros’ love had made them the oldest and wisest of races. Let the younger races come, and claim the world, for when it is all said and done, it shall be the Elves who outlive them all.
