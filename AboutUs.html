About Us

   The Blackfeather Adventuring Company, occasionally referred to as Blackfeather International, is the oldest and largest professional adventuring company in the world. Though its founding records are officially sealed, it is suspected that the Company was founded around a century ago just after the creation of Nova island. The time frame leads some to believe that it was founded by one of the Adventuring group responsible for that event. It is rumored that since the CEO of Blackfeather never makes public appearances and his identity is secret, it is still headed by the same adventurer.


    Regardless, Blackfeather has been around long enough to have been giving equal degrees of respect and suspicion by the international community. Blackfeather insists that they are a legal resource of professional specialists who can be combined and instructed to solve any number of unique, dangerous, or unusual problems on an extremely rapid timeframe, problems that larger more traditional forces would be helpless to combat. Their critics claim they are nothing more than flashy mercenaries, and sometimes that is true. They often serve as hired muscle, pulling guard duty and occasionally deploying large forces.


    Blackfeather, however, does not participate in matters of war and do not accept contracts that they feel would violate the sovereignty of any nation. They have fallen into grey areas numerous times, but generally maintain their position as a neutral force. Blackfeather, for its part, does its best to put a trackable and accountable face on adventuring. In the past, hiring adventurers was a dicey proposition only used in desperate circumstances, as hiring a group of powerful yahoos and hoping they did what you asked could end up causing more problems than it solves.

   

    Blackfeather Adventurers work on officially agreed contracts, negotiated by Blackfeather itself. If something goes wrong, Blackfeather recalls the adventurers. Any legal action is taken against the Company itself and not the adventurers. While Blackfeather has a crack legal team, they generally prefer to settle under the table and not let things get out of hand. However, if a client gives a bad contract that violates Blackfeather's rules, or betrays its adventurers, Blackfeather's legal team can get truly vicious in ensuring the safety of its reputation and its adventurers.


    Blackfeather has been enormously successful. The degree of validity and safety provided has not only ensured many lucrative contracts, it has also attracted numerous intelligent, resourceful, and powerful adventurers. It is worth noting that many other Adventuring Companies exist, and freelance Adventuring is still a viable path. Blackfeather in fact prefers their adventurers to have previous experience before applying, especially in a structured adventuring environment.


    In the end, most see Blackfeather as a necessary grey area. On one hand, they are an extremely powerful group that is beholden to apparently no one but themselves and their pocketbooks. On the other hand, well, as their advertising insists: If you need that Dragon kicked out of that castle in a rapid and orderly manner, there's no one better to call.
