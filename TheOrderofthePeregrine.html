The Order of the Peregrine

    The Order of the Peregrine is an extremist human-supremacist organization based in the former Empire of Lock. They are an extremely secretive and extremely dangerous organization. They believe that the old empire collapsed due to the arrival of foreigners, non-humans, and other "degenerates." They believe that the only way to bring back the might of the old city states is to weed out these "undesirables" so that the natural superiority of humans can shine through. It is important to note that they also believe that their should only be one human culture- the central culture that dominated the old Empire of Lock. They view the wide array of various different human cultures that exist as a form of degeneracy- only by uniting as the true old culture can they rise to superiority again. They take their name from the old symbol of the Lock family line- a peregrine falcon.


    The truly dangerous thing about the Order of the Peregrine is how they operate. They operate in compartmentalized cells- One small group does not know anything about other groups that operate in their area. They only have contacts with their organizers and handlers, who are usually separate from any violent action and very difficult to capture. They are an organization united by belief in a cause, as opposed to reward and central leadership. 

 

   Unfortunately, the leadership they have are well-connected and many humans are secretly sympathetic towards their goals. It is theorized that their higher leadership is mostly wealthy humans from old noble families that have ties to the old Empire of Lock. These people are well respected in their communities, and may even have law enforcement in their pockets. Since it is hard to prove membership, this makes them nearly immune.


    Order members have no delusions that they will ever been seen as heroes. To them, it is not necessary. They know that they will be hated and pursued, and they know that their heinous actions will never be accepted. For them, this is acceptable. They believe that regardless of whether or not they are in command, the natural superiority of humans will come to the front if only they can weed out the degenerates. They do not care if a large number a humans are killed in the process. This is what truly makes them dangerous, as they will do nearly anything to achieve their goals no matter how horrifying.



SPECIAL NOTE:

Blackfeather adventurers are noted to take extreme caution if they believe the Order is operating in their area. Senior analysts indicate that the Order of the Peregrine has a special hatred for the diverse Blackfeather, and view them as one of the primary groups that must be eliminated. Their have been several Peregrine attacks on Blackfeather members in the past. Up until recently, we thought of them as just another hate group who burn shops and chase out non-humans. They have recently demonstrated that they have far more resources and are far more ruthless than we believed. Do not underestimate them.
