Serabeth

SERABETH

Lawful Good

Portfolio: Justice, Alignment, Lost Causes, Restraint, Feuding, Honor

Domains: Good, Law, Destruction, Protection, Glory, Nobility

Subdomains: Rage, Archon (Lawful Good), Purity, Honor, Heroism, Defense

Preferred Weapon: Longsword

Serabeth, the divine judge, had a grand plan for her Chosen.  She gave them the gift of Temperance, of correct judgement, justice, self-control, abstention, and prudence.  When Bellaphex twisted the Gifts of her fellow deities, her people's repression and abstention lead to their failure to thrive as a race in the desperate early years of the world.  This began her thorough and unending hatred of all things twisted by the influence of Sin.  She mourned the loss of her Chosen by offering her Gift to any and all who would take up the cause of her feud.  This feud would never end.
After offering her Gift to all, Serabeth codified the formal alignment system so that her new Chosen followers would be able to easily determine saints from Bellaphex's twisted sinners.  Her new Chosen choose her instead of the other way around, finding that her mindset and ideology lead them to her.  This means that most of her new Chosen are paladins.  Her feud against Evil will not end until it is eliminated from the world entirely.
There is some debate as to whether or not Serabeth is or was a greater deity. Her followers argue that she once had Chosen, and is therefore a greater deity. Others argue that she doesn't have any Chosen left and has therefore fallen, or argue that she never had Chosen in the first place. Due to her popularity among many law-abiding organizations, however, she is generally left on many orthodox greater deity lists.
