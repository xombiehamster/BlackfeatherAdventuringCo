The Walking Ancestors

The Walking Ancestors


Neutral Good
    The Walking Ancestors are the whole of the collected elven ancestors honored as a group. The conceptual group functions as a minor deity in that it does provide clerics, paladins, and inquisitors with magical abilities in the name of the collected group. Deathless divine casters, as members of the Walking Ancestors, do not, in fact, grant themselves their magical powers, and must still maintain reverence for the Ancestors as a whole. The Ancestors recognize their ties to Hekahuh, and do not hold animosity toward negatively aligned undead, but do not include him in their worship. Similarly, the Ancestors recognize their ties to Calestros, but do not embrace her chaotic nature. The Ancestors do accept that their worship does stem from her Gift of Pride, and does include her in their number, but places her among the Ancestors, not above them. This is the central heresy of the cult of Walking Ancestors which separates the cult from other elven sects, keeping their numbers relatively small.
    The Ancestors, like Hekahuh do not make any claim as to what, precisely, the afterlife (if one exists) actually entails.

Preferred Weapon: Longsword

Domains

Death, Elf, Good, Knowledge
    As honored ancestors, the Walking Ancestors as a concept is one powerfully tied to the positive energy of Good. The Walking Ancestors are distinctly elven, making Elf one of their major domains. It is also very strongly tied to undeath, and thus Death is also a major domain. Because of their long historical memories, the Ancestors' last domain is Knowledge.

Subdomains

Undeath, Memory, Ancestors
    As the Walking Ancestors are made up of undead elves, Undeath is one of the granted subdomains. Ancestors, usually a subdomain associated with Repose, is here moved into the Death domain for purposes of the Ancestors' understanding. Ancestors are revered but expected to be up and moving about instead of being left to a quiet eternity. With the length of elven lifespans and the extreme length of undead existences, Memory is also an available subdomain. Even elves who do not revere the Ancestors will go to them for accurate clarifications of historical events.
