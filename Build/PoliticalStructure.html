Political Structure

The colony is run under a system of near-direct democracy.  At its head is the Governor, advised by the State Counsel.  The State Counsel is made up of three minority population representatives, one mummy, one lich, and one deathless counselor; three political representatives, one religious leader, the head of the military, and the external ambassador; and the mayor of each township, excluding the capitol, Two Rivers.


The mayor of each city is elected directly by the people of the city.  Each city may govern itself as it chooses, but must follow the rules set by State overall in its dealings with outside governments, trade, and issues of social justice.  No city may infringe on the right of any individual to live, die, or unlive as their choice so long as that individual is not committing any actual crime.  Being evil or being good is not in itself ever to be considered a crime.  No formerly-living persons may be raised as intelligent undead without their consent.  No unliving persons can be resurrected to life without their consent.  It is not legal to magically control the sentient living impaired.  Because of the sanctions on magical control of will it is also illegal to own or otherwise restrict the will of both living and living impaired persons.  This does mean that within the UNENS slavery is illegal.


When it comes to institutional momentum, no society has it ingrained quite as deeply as the UNENS.  While there are elections every half decade, the same candidates are typically running, with the same people voting for them, with the same results every time.  As such, though a century has passed, the Governor is still Redfarn Betcher (though now undead himself), and the State Counsel still contains the mummy Yousul er-Rashal, the lich Kas, and the Undying Counselor Alsaith Eldair.  The religious leader, previously Divino Bruglioni High Priest of Nerull, has been replaced with Bronte Doneto, the High Priest of Hekahuh.  Helspeth Blackboots has retired, and her position as the external ambassador has been re-filled with a Forged of uncertain lineage.  The necropolitan Pinkus Ghort was promoted to Head of the Military after Piper Hect's retirement.  Captain Hect's daughter has earned Captain Ghort's previous title of second in command.  With these changes of position being handed to non-living persons, the counsel has stabilized and not changed in decades.
