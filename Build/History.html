History

    Thousands of years ago, the Lycan Empire was a mighty nation fueled by brutal conquest, spanning several continents. However, a combined army of Elves, Humans, and Halflings drove them back to the remote northern mountains and shattered their empire. In the centuries that followed, the Lycans splintered into different tribes categorized by species. Eventually, they were a pale shadow of their former selves, scattered and disorganized.


    That changed with the founding of the ALT. Lord Valdyr the Wise, a werewolf, formed an alliance between 12 of the largest tribes and set out to the newly-created Nova Island to start a true Lycanthropic nation. The city of New Skaplyndi was formed, and Under Valdyr's leadership the ALT made allies out of their new neighbors on the island and ended up having significant military might despite their small size.


    The ALT itself is closest in government to a republic. Since news of the success and creation of the ALT has spread around the world, a staggering variety of Lycanthropes have made their homes in the ALT. The individual tribes have mostly morphed into political blocks who elect a Chief to the Cabal, the nation's equivalent of a parliament. The Cabal is headed by a Lord, currently still an aging Valdyr. The nation's politics is still in flux, so it's not clear exactly how much power the Lord has. Valdyr wants it clear that the real power is with the Cabal, and that the Lord is mostly a balance. However, his charisma and tendency to personally solve problems frequently runs counter to this sentiment. With no clear heir, the nation is still much under Valdyr's sway.


    Valdyr's full title is "Drottin Valdyr Vitru," the chiefs are "Yfirmadr," and the Cabal is called the "Jofnudr." These are only really relevant if one actually speaks Skaplyndi, which is a hard language to learn.


    The ALT itself is mostly rural. The ALT claimed a large portion of forest and fertile land on the western side of the island. When the Thaumaturgist settlement collapsed and the Ork hordes were driven out, the ALT claimed rulership of these territories. With no other nations bordering the areas except the GUILD Dwarves, who weren't really willing to settle a swamp, the ALT took de-facto control of a huge swath of territory.

 

   There are only a handful of true cities in the ALT, the capital New Skaplyndi being one of them. Most of the population prefers to live in scattered farming or mining communities. Since the founding, Valdyr has pressed a continuing campaign of modernization. Education is surprisingly high for such a rural population, and most Lycans are by no means primitive. Some, however, do not want to abandon a tribal life, but also want to participate in an organized nation. This is how the ALT's unique military was formed.
