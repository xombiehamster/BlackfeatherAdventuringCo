Bellaphex

BELLAPHEX 

Chaotic Evil

Portfolio: Monsters, Corruption, Destruction, Disaster, Slaughter, Terror, Innovation, Sins
Domains: Chaos, Death, Destruction, Evil, Luck, War, Weather
Subdomains: Catastrophe, Murder, Fear, Curse, Blood, Storms

Preferred Weapon: Greatsword

Bellaphex, the patron of Monsters, has always had a grand plan against all Chosen. While other gods created and coddled the species of the World, exemplifying this or blessing them with that or so on, she looked on with naked disgust. The creatures they cooed over were weak things, fragile and pitiful. She would have liked nothing better than to see them scoured from the world, leaving nothing behind. But Bellaphex was no Creator: The very idea rankled her. But instead, she saw great potential in what resided on the World, in the very species the other gods had created and exalted. She corrupted them, twisting their forms and minds, giving them pleasure in slaughter, a bloodlust unquenchable. Where her corruption of form and thought could not take hold, she brought a corruption of the soul, twisting the Gifts the others had sought to give their creations. The Pride of Elves, meant to give them solace, would make them Aloof and disdainful of the other races. The Envy of Humans, meant to make them ever striving, would make them bitter and distrustful of the other races, and even themselves. The Gluttony of the Goblins, meant to make them desire the new and exciting, would make them gorge themselves to excess.

It is this that was the Gift of Bellaphex, given to those she had made to be Her Chosen: The Gift of Sins, of corruption and decay, that all other Gifts would be turned against those who received them. Weakened by that which was meant to give them strength, the Monsters, Murderers, and Villains of the World would be given free reign to strike against them and themselves, creating a World of Ankle deep blood.

It was thus that Orcs gave rise to Orks: it was thus that Vampires could create Spawn: it was thus that Giants became Trolls: it was thus that Bugbears became Ogres: it was thus that Abberations came into the World, and all their ilk.
