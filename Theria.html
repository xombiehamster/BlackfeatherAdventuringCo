Theria

THERIA 

Lawful Good
Portfolio: Animals, Evolution, Innocence, Nature, Plants, Survival, Mindlessness, Wrath
Domains: Animal, Good, Plant, Strength, Sun, Weather
Subdomains: Feather, Fur, Ferocity, Day, Light, Seasons, Storms

Preferred Weapon: Natural Attacks
Theria, Patron of The World’s Animals, has no grand plan for her Chosen. The first of all the gods to gaze upon The World, she was immediately enraptured by it: already, it bore a complex system of growth and survival amongst all the creatures that inhabited it. As the other gods looked over The World, seeing all the kinds of people who would emerge upon it, and choosing favorites from among them, Theria instead took keen interest in those the others overlooked: the birds, fish, mammals, reptiles, and all the other non-sapients The World had to offer. Indeed, in those ancient times, many of those who would become Chosen still numbered among them; Where the other gods thought only of future worshippers, she saw the animals and nature of the world as a great tapestry, eager and pliant to her influence. She admired their brutal simplicity, their endless, ferocious battle to survive, both against each other and against The World they lived in. By changing one small element of an environment, the creatures there would, in their struggle to survive, adapt to a new, brilliant form. With wild and eager abandon, she made her mark on The World, shaping fantastic and majestic creatures, some of elegant beauty, some of ferocious strength, all part of a magnificent web of constantly shifting, interrelating patterns of hunter and hunted, predator and prey, self-regulating, yet always following the same basic rules she had found when first she gazed upon the world. Those rules, that animalistic drive, this thoughtless fury, the heedless struggle to survive, no matter what, this Wrath was Theria’s gift to her Chosen; While those the other gods had chosen would come to burden themselves with concepts like law, society, and morals, her chosen would remain free of such shackles. But unlike the others, Theria was not a jealous god: although the Chosen of The World changed to become more than mere animals, Theria never stopped loving them like her own. Despite their new trappings, deep within each of them her Gift, her Wrath, still remains, waiting beneath the surface to emerge and protect each of them in their time of need. Unlike the others, she never has to fear another’s dominance, for her Gift will live on no matter what...
